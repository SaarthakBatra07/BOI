PERMANENT STAFFING

Our executive recruiting consultants possess the expertise and contacts to best support our search. We draw on our high-level professional networks, industry knowledge and internal research resources to identify the right people. To stay on the cutting edge of talent strategy, we constantly track key trends in the global market for talent, and continually innovate our services and approach.

RECRUITMENT PROCESS OUTSOURCING (RPO)

Our RPO solutions are designed to enable you to fully outsource your Talent Acquisition function to us.

RECRUITMENT DIP STIK (RDS)

An independent recruitment ‘Health-Check’ is conducted by an expert which serves as an independent verification of policy, process & people to drive changes in a company's recruitment function.

ARMED FORCES SEARCH (AFS)

A separate service for placement of armed forces candidates to various industries.

TEMPORARY STAFFING (TEMPS)

Our temporary staffing services will allow your company to staff up or down depending on the needs of your current business cycle. Temporary staffing allows you to bring down your costs on hiring, training, and providing benefits to new employees, along with the administrative costs of the human resources department. It also lets you give a respite to your permanent staff who may be overloaded during busy periods.

EMPLOYEE LEASING (EMP)

Looking to recruit new talent without getting bogged down by the hassles of HR management? Employee leasing solutions may be your best choice to get new hires on board swiftly and painlessly.
